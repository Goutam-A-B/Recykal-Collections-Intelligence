Automated Payment Reminder System
The system identifies invoices approaching or crossing due dates and triggers reminder emails. Large segment customers are excluded from automated overdue notices. Reminders are deduplicated per (shipment_id, reminder_type, due_date).

Reminder Business Rules

7-Day ReminderSent 7 days before invoice due date to prompt early payment.
3-Day ReminderSent 3 days before due date as a follow-up warning.
1-Day Final ReminderSent 1 day before due date as final notice.
Overdue NoticeSent after due date if outstanding > 0. Large segment excluded.
DeduplicationEach (shipment, type, due_date) combination is sent only once.

Pipeline Architecture

Phase 1 — Load & ValidateLoad customers, shipments, payments CSVs. Validate data integrity.
Phase 2 — Compute ReceivablesFIFO payment allocation per customer. Calculate outstanding per shipment.
Phase 3 — Reminders (A-1)Generate payment reminders based on due date windows. Deduplication via log.
Phase 4 — Monthly (A-2)Generate monthly balance confirmations per customer. One email per customer.
Phase 5 — Dashboard (B)Generate interactive HTML dashboard + Excel workbook for Google Sheets.
AutomationGitHub Actions cron runs every 15 min. Commits refreshed artifacts to deploy/.
